Important Notice

Clippings NOT Compatible With Thunderbird 78

Thunderbird 78 will be the next major release of this email client, and is expected to be released soon.

At this time, Clippings will not be compatible with Thunderbird 78.

I want to keep using Clippings. What should I do?

If you want to keep using Clippings for Thunderbird, consider staying on Thunderbird 68.

To do that, you'll need to turn off automatic updates for Thunderbird. If you don't, your Thunderbird installation may automatically update once Thunderbird 78 is released, which will cause Clippings to stop working.

Follow these steps:

  1. Click the Thunderbird menu (â˜° icon) and then select Options → Options.
  2. On the left navigation sidebar, click Advanced.
  3. Click on the Update tab, then click the radio button with the title, "Check for updates, but let me choose whether to install them"

But I want to upgrade to the latest version of Thunderbird. What will happen to Clippings?

If you upgrade to Thunderbird 78 when it is released, then Clippings for Thunderbird will stop working, and it will no longer be available to use.

It is strongly recommended that you export your clippings as soon as possible so that you have a copy for safekeeping as well as for import to other applications. To export your clippings, open Clippings Manager, and then click Tools → Export.

When exporting, choose both the Clippings 6 and CSV (comma-separated values) formats.
